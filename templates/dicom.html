Dicom
Delivering Confident Communication


Maharishi Vidya Mandir introduced Dicom school programme for the students and a few students have opted this programme at the Primary Level. The classes are held twice in a week for each section. Special trainers from the managemen handle these classes. It focuses how to speak english fluently.It cover speed reading and speaking without any mistake.Dicom teaches adopt many techniques that will support children to face the challenges in life.

Head office is in chennai . Zonal office is in krishnagiri.There are 8 trainers 1 leader.
