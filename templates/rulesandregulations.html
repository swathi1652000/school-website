RULES & REGULATIONS

1. All the students should come in the prescribed and neat uniform every day during the school hours and to all school functions.

2. Nobody should bring mobile phones or any other gadgets.

3. During school hours parents/guardians are not allowed to see their wards or their teachers in the classroom without the permission of the principal.

4. Any communications, request or complaint made by the parents/guardians should be addressed only to the principal and not to the teacher in the respective classes.

5. Any wilful damage caused by the student to the building, furniture, apparatus or any other school property is liable to be made good by the parent/guardian.

6. Student who is ill for a long period of time must bring a Medical Certificate.

7. Students should be punctual to school and bring the prescribed books according to the timetable.

8. Students will be eligible to sit for examination only if they put in 80% of attendance.

9. Homework assigned by the teachers should be done regularly and parents/guardians are requested to co-operate with the school authorities by checking the homework of their wards.
